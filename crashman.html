CRASH MAN

ABOUT

Crash Man is a bomb-based Robot Master who uses explosive attacks and high damage projectiles.

TIPS & TRICKS

  • Use Air Shooter for effective damage.
  • Stay at a safe distance from explosions.
  • Watch for sudden bomb jumps.
  • Keep moving to avoid blast radius.
