FLASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

ABOUT

Flash Man can stop time briefly, making him one of the most dangerous Robot Masters in Mega Man 2.

TIPS & TRICKS

  • Use Crash Bomb for strong damage.
  • Move carefully when time is slowed.
  • Avoid direct hit patterns.
  • Don’t panic during time stop phases.
