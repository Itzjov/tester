ABOUT

Heat Man is a fire-based Robot Master from Mega Man 2. His stage includes intense heat hazards, fire traps, and moving platforms.

TIPS & TRICKS

  • Use Bubble Lead for maximum damage.
  • Avoid standing near fire blocks for too long.
  • Time jumps carefully in heated sections.
  • Stay mobile to avoid flame bursts.
